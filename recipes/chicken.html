Chicken Recipe

This actually uses my breading for Chicken Fried Steak---my husband suggested we try it with chicken breasts, and it was delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup flourr
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs bone in

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Make marination/li>
  3. Mix in flour
  4. Turn onto a lightly floured surface
  5. Place chicken on oven pan
  6. Put in oven and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees
